# Ingress Controllers

**Ingress** defines HTTP/HTTPS routes (host, path, TLS) to **Services**. An **Ingress controller** (NGINX, Traefik, cloud LB) implements those rules—like a reverse proxy in front of your Pods.

> Similar to putting **Traefik/nginx** in front of multiple `docker compose` services, but route config is Kubernetes-native YAML.
