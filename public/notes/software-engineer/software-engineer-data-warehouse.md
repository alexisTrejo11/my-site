# Data Warehouse

> Structured, curated store optimized for SQL analytics and BI.

## What it is

- Star/snowflake schemas, [Columnar Storage](/learning/software-engineer-columnar-storage).
- Examples: Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift.
- ETL/ELT from operational DBs and lakes.

## Why it matters

Understanding **Data Warehouse** helps you reason about trade-offs in production data systems — when to apply it, what breaks at scale, and how it connects to neighboring ideas in this section.

## In practice

- Map the concept to tools you use (PostgreSQL, Kafka, Redis, etc.).
- Look for metrics or symptoms that indicate misuse or missing optimization.
- Cross-read the section index: [MOC](/learning/devops-cloud).
